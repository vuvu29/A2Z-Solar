Common Questions

What is your response time for customer inquiries?

We strive to respond to all customer inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical support issues, we typically respond within 4 hours.

Do you offer onsite consultations?

Yes, we offer free onsite consultations for solar installations. Our experts will visit your property to assess your needs and provide a customized solution.

Can I schedule an appointment outside of business hours?

We offer limited evening and weekend appointments for consultations. Please contact our scheduling department to check availability.

What should I bring to my consultation appointment?

For onsite consultations, please have your recent electricity bills available and any information about your roof (age, material, etc.). For showroom visits, just bring your questions and energy goals!

Do you service areas outside Energy City?

Yes, we service the entire metropolitan area and surrounding regions. Contact us to check if we serve your specific location.
