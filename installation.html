Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical residential installation take?

Most residential installations are completed within 3-5 business days, depending on system size and complexity. This includes mounting, electrical work, and final inspections.

Do you help with permits and incentives?

Yes! We handle all necessary permits for your installation and provide complete documentation for federal, state, and local solar incentives and tax credits.

What warranties do you offer?

We offer a 10-year workmanship warranty on all installations, in addition to manufacturer warranties on equipment (typically 25 years for panels, 10-12 years for inverters).

Can I add batteries to my system later?

Absolutely! We design systems with future expansion in mind. Adding battery storage later is straightforward with our pre-planned systems.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with several financial institutions to offer competitive financing options, including $0-down plans for qualified homeowners.
